Trenton Fletcher

I'm a mechanical engineering graduate with a passion for building and refining electromechanical systems. After four years in the U.S. Air Force maintaining complex avionics on B-52 bombers, I transitioned to engineering with a focus on controls, embedded systems, and rapid prototyping. While completing my degree at the University of Washington I became a core member of the Design Build Fly team, leading high-speed propulsion development and wind tunnel testing, and helping us place 3rd in the 2023-2024 competition.

I have a deep curiosity for how things work and a drive to improve them. I've built everything from amatuer rockets to thermal-tracking RC vehicles, and I'm always exploring new tools and technologies. (My 3D printer is one of the best investments I've ever made.) I also hold a B.A. in psychology and philosophy from the University of Vermont, which continues to shape how I approach systems, teams, and problem-solving.

This website is both a portfolio and a personal project. I built it from scratch to share what I've worked on and where I'm headed next.
